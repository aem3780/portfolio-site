Digital Employee Experience: Bubble

About the project:

  • Software: Figma, FigJam
  • Skills: UX/UI design and research
  • Duration: 7 weeks
  • Team members: 6 designers

The problem:

With the growing prevalence of remote workplaces, the Digital Employee Experience (DEX) has become an increasingly stressful and highly criticized part of the typical work experience. Despite remote work becoming more normalized, employers and employees struggle to communicate and adapt in this rapidly changing environment.

The solution:

We wanted to create a system that adheres to these principles and display them using the theme of a bubble: a simple shape that can interact with others of its kind.

Project Goals:

Our team's main goal when creating Bubble was to design a system that establishes accountability, equity, effective communication, healthy boundaries, and embraces diverse work styles in the workplace.
